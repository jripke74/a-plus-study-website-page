3 Hardware

3.1 Display Types

LCD

  • Liquid crystal display
    • Light shines through liquid crystals
  • Advantages
    • Lightweight
    • Relatively low power
    • Relatively inexpensive
  • Disadvantages
    • Black levels are a challenge
    • Requiress separate backlight
    • Florescent, LED, etc.
    • Lights are difficult to replace

LCD technologies

  • TN (Twisted Nematic) LCD
    • The original LCD technology
    • Fast response times (gaming!)
    • Poor viewing angles - color shifts
  • IPS (In Plane Switching) LCD
    • Excelent color representation
    • Can be more expensive to produce that TN
  • VA (Vertical Alignment) LCD
    • A good compromise between TN and IPS
    • Good color representation
    • Often slower response times than TN
